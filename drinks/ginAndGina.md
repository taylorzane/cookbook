# Gin and Orangina
## Ingredients
- 2 parts gin
  - Personal Favorites:
    - Bombay Sapphire London Dry Gin
    - Tanqueray London Dry Gin
  - Others to Try:
    - Hendrick's Gin
- 4 parts Orangina
- Ice

## Procedure
- Combine gin and Orangina in shaker.
- Add ice, and shake.
- Pour into tall glass, on the rocks, or straight up.
- Enjoy.

# SoCo Cherry, Coke, and Lime
## Ingredients
- 1 part Southern Comfort Cherry liqueur
- 3 parts Coca-Cola
  - Coca-Cola with Lime works well as a replacement.
- 1/4 part lime juice

## Preparation
- Combine SoCo, Coke, and lime juice in mixer.
- Add ice, and shake.
- Pour into an old-fashioned glass, on the rocks.
- Enjoy.

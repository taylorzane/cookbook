# Tom Collins
## Ingredients
- 3 parts gin
  - Personal Favorites:
    - Bombay Sapphire London Dry Gin
    - Tanqueray London Dry Gin
  - Others to Try:
    - Hendrick's Gin
- 2 parts lime juice
  - I prefer the taste of lime over lemon.
- 1 part sugar syrup
  - If no simple syrup:
    - 1 part water
    - 1 part sugar
- 4 parts club soda

## Procedure
- Add gin, lemon juice and sugar syrup in a tall glass.
- Add ice, and stir.
- Top up with club soda.
- Enjoy.
